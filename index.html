Welcome To

Energy and Environment Conservation Club

EEC is a Club In KEC, Perundurai. We focus on conserving energy and environment

Our Motto

Save Energy

Switching to renewable energy in your household. Turning off the lights when you leave a room. Using energy-efficient lightbulbs.

Save Environment

Follow the three "R's" to conserve natural resources and landfill space. Cut down on what you throw away and use natural resources wisely, in order to avoid waste.

No Wastage

Zero Waste: The conservation of all resources by means of responsible production, consumption, reuse and recovery of all products.

How We Started

About

The National Energy Conservation Day is observed on December 14th. We started Our Club on this day to make people aware of global warming and climate change and promotes efforts towards saving energy resources.
